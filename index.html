About Me

I'm a computer science student in my 3rd year at the
University of Texas Rio Grande Valley.


Some of my interests include machine learning, web development, and robotics! This summer I worked under the OSU Share Lab as an undergraduate researcher. I worked on designing and implementing robotic systems to promote the physical and mental wellbeing of the elder population in Skilled Nursing Facilities (SNF) with SAMI the robot.
